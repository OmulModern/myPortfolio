About Me

I have a dream! That we transform the world. I wish that we, the people all over the world, take conscious steps, as a whole, to rearrange society and the way we live on earth so that it works for the benefit and well-being of all people everywhere. It is possible and even inevitable to build a completely new form of society, truly civilized and intelligent, in which money does not exist and everything is free and open.

I support a Resource Based Economy proposed by The Venus Project founded by the extraordinary mind and vast life experience of Jacque Fresco. Researching the lifelong work of Jacque Fresco I was inspired, and so, out of a desire to help make the world a better place, I began to learn to code by creating websites to spread awareness about a new way of thinking and a world of free access.
